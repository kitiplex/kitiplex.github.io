### Start here

<details>
<summary>IIG</summary>

Get as much info as possible.

```md
* When did you first notice this problem?

* When was the last time it worked properly?

* Have there been any changes made before the issue started?

* Is this happening repeatedly?

* What steps have you taken to try and fix it?

* How much is this affecting your work?

** Are there any other users experiencing the same issue?

** Are you still able to perform your tasks, or are you using a spare machine?
```

</details>

```md
Hi ,

Thanks for reaching out to us!

Your request has been placed in the queue and will be worked on by the next available technician.

Kindly, let us know if the issue/request submitted has significantly affected your work performance and we'll prioritize it in our queue.

Thank you for your patience.

Best,
```


</details>
