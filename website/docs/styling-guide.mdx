# Website Styling Guide: Elevate Your User Experience

Welcome to our **website styling guide**! This document is designed to ensure consistency and a seamless user experience across our digital platforms. By adhering to these styling guidelines, we aim to create a visually appealing, user-friendly, and cohesive online presence.

## Color Palette:

__Primary color :__
<Highlight color="#0058A0">Deep blue</Highlight>

__Secondary color :__
<Highlight color="#FF4500">Vibrant Orange</Highlight>

__Accent Color :__
<Highlight color="#41B883">Fresh Green</Highlight>

__Background color :__
<Highlight color="#F5F5F5">Light Gray</Highlight>



Maintain consistency in color usage to establish brand identity. Use primary colors for headings, secondary for call-to-action elements, and accent colors for highlights.

## Typography:

- **Heading Font:** Montserrat
- **Body Font:** Open Sans
- **Font Sizes:**
  - H1: 36px
  - H2: 28px
  - H3: 24px
  - Body Text: 16px

Ensure legibility by maintaining appropriate font sizes. Use bold or italic styles sparingly for emphasis.

## Layout:

- **Grid System:** 12-column layout
- **Margins and Padding:**
  - Standard Margin: 16px
  - Standard Padding: 20px

Adopt a responsive design approach, ensuring that the layout adjusts seamlessly across various devices. Utilize the grid system for consistent alignment.

## Images:

- **File Format:** PNG for logos, JPEG for photographs
- **Optimal Resolution:** 1920x1080 pixels
- **Alt Text:** Provide descriptive alt text for accessibility

Optimize images for fast loading times and responsiveness. Alt text is crucial for users with disabilities and enhances SEO.

## Buttons and Links:

- **Button Style:** Rounded corners, solid primary color with white text
- **Link Style:** Underlined on hover, secondary color
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle color change or shadow effect

Create intuitive navigation by making buttons and links visually distinguishable. Implement hover effects for a more engaging experience.

## Forms:

- **Input Fields:** Use a light background with dark text
- **Button:** Follow the button styling guidelines
- **Error Messages:** Display in red with clear instructions

Ensure that forms are easy to complete and submit. Clearly indicate errors with concise messages.

## Icons:

- **Icon Style:** Simple line icons
- **Color:** Primary or secondary color

Icons should be easily recognizable and enhance user understanding. Consistency in icon style contributes to a unified visual language.

## Accessibility:

- **Contrast Ratio:** Ensure a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for text
- **Keyboard Navigation:** Test and optimize for keyboard navigation
- **Alt Text:** Provide descriptive alt text for all images

Prioritize accessibility to make your website inclusive and user-friendly for all audiences.

Remember, these guidelines serve as a foundation for a cohesive and visually appealing website. Regularly review and update styles as needed to stay current with design trends and user expectations. Happy designing!
